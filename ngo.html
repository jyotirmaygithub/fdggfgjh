2. Forrest India

FORREST – Forest Regeneration and Environmental Sustainability Trust, was born on OCT 2015. It is registered as a not-for-profit organization (NGO). We conceptualized FORREST to strengthen the bond between humans and nature and to create a path for harmonious co-existence. We are committed to work for large scale habitat restorations for biodiversity conservation, ecosystem connectivity and resilience towards climate change. We believe in collective wisdom. We believe our vision can be attained through action-oriented positive interventions, awareness and advocacy. We aspire to re-establish bond between man and nature.
