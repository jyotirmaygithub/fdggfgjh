CONSTITUTIONAL ACTS

SECTION-21 of hte Maharashtra preservation of trees act, 1971, says about the punishment of cutting down the trees is a fine of rupees of 1000 to rupees 5000 along with imprisonment ranging from one weak to one year.

According to the indian forest act, the penality for cutting down a tree is rs 10000 or 3 months imprisonment. This punishment may extend up to one year under various state acts.

SECTION 33 Indian Forest Act

Penalites for acts in contravention of notification under section 30.

Any person who fells any tree reserved under section 30, or strips off the bark or leaves from, or otherwise damages, any such tree, permits cattle to damage any such tree shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term which may to extend to six months, or with fine which may extend to five hundred rupees, or with both.
